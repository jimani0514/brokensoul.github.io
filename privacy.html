Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains how Double J Labs ("we", "our", or "us") collects, uses, shares, and protects information when you use Broken Soul, our mobile game, websites, and related services (the "Service").

This policy is intended to help explain our data practices, including the data handled by third-party services and SDKs used in the Service. It does not constitute legal advice.

1. Information We Collect

Depending on how you use the Service, we may collect or process the following categories of information:

  • Account and authentication information: Google login identifiers, user IDs, email address if provided through authentication, and related account metadata.
  • Game data: gameplay progress, rankings, scores, rewards, inventory, preferences, language settings, purchase-related game state, and other information needed to provide and maintain the Service.
  • Purchase information: in-app purchase records and purchase validation information handled through Google Play Billing.
  • Device or other identifiers: Android Advertising ID, Firebase identifiers, app instance IDs, FCM push notification tokens, App Check tokens, Play Integrity tokens, and similar identifiers used for app functionality, analytics, advertising, security, and fraud prevention.
  • Analytics and diagnostics: app usage events, ad interaction data, crash or diagnostic information, performance information, and technical information such as device model, operating system, app version, and network-related metadata.
  • Support information: information you provide when you contact us for customer support, account deletion, or other requests.

2. How We Use Information

We use collected information for the following purposes:

  • Provide, operate, maintain, and improve the Service.
  • Authenticate users and manage game accounts.
  • Save and synchronize gameplay progress, rankings, rewards, preferences, and related game data.
  • Process, validate, and support in-app purchases through Google Play Billing.
  • Provide rewarded ads and other advertising features, measure ad performance, and prevent ad abuse.
  • Analyze app usage, improve gameplay experience, diagnose technical issues, and measure service performance.
  • Protect the Service, prevent fraud, detect abuse, enforce rules, and support security and compliance.
  • Send push notifications where enabled by the user.
  • Provide information about game benefits, rewards, events, promotions, updates, future titles, sequels, and other game-related marketing communications, where permitted by law and where any required consent has been obtained.
  • Respond to customer support, account deletion, and legal requests.

3. Third-Party Services and Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We may share or allow processing of information with service providers and SDKs that help us operate the Service. These providers may collect or process information directly from your device as part of their services.

These services may include:

  • Google Play Services: authentication support, device services, app functionality, and security-related services.
  • Google Mobile Ads / AdMob: advertising delivery, rewarded ads, ad measurement, fraud prevention, and related advertising services.
  • Firebase: analytics, app performance, App Check, Cloud Messaging, and related app infrastructure services.
  • Supabase: authentication, database, game data storage, server functions, and related backend services.
  • Google Play Billing: in-app purchase processing, purchase validation, and payment-related services.

We may also disclose information if required by law, to protect our rights, to prevent fraud or abuse, or to comply with legal obligations.

4. Device Identifiers, Advertising, and Analytics

The Service uses device or other identifiers, including Android Advertising ID, Firebase identifiers, app instance IDs, FCM push tokens, App Check tokens, and Play Integrity tokens. These identifiers are used for app functionality, analytics, advertising, ad measurement, security, fraud prevention, and compliance.

In the current version of the Service, these device, advertising, and analytics identifiers are collected as required for app operation because the Service does not provide a complete in-app opt-out that stops all related SDK collection. Users can control certain device-level settings provided by Android or Google, such as resetting or limiting advertising identifiers where available.

You may request deletion of your account and associated data through in-game settings where available or by contacting us at support@brokensoul.xyz. We may need to retain certain information where required by law, for fraud prevention, dispute resolution, security, or legitimate business purposes.
