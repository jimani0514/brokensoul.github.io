The game

Read the lane. Break the boss.

Broken Soul turns Soulslike pressure into grid-based 2D action. Dodge, parry, read attack patterns, find the safe lane, and counter when the boss finally leaves a wound in its rhythm.

01

Grid-based combat

Episode 1 plays in a 3x2 vertical mobile grid. Episode 2 expands into a 5x2 PC grid with 16:9 remastered boss arenas.

02

Timing, dodge, parry

The fight is decided by timing and pattern reading, not button mashing.

03

Bosses without filler

More than twenty boss-type monsters turn every stage into a test of nerve, memory, and precision.

04

Two combat classes

Nyra and Jiny carry different reach, tempo, and risk so each rematch asks for a new kind of courage.

Series by platform

One world, two chapters.

The same broken soul is split across two forms of play: a vertical mobile chapter built for quick, sharp trials, and a remastered PC chapter built for wider arenas and deeper boss mastery.
